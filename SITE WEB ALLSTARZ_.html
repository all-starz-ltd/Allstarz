About All-Starz_ltd

All-Starz_ltd is an independent label and production house founded and run by Alan ACQUART. We make records the messy way — honest, loud, lo‑fi when it needs to be. From punk‑leaning indie to fragile folk moments, we help artists capture the take that feels like truth. present me your project and I'll listen carefully. cheers duzettes and duzes
